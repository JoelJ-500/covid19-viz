Data Insights & Representation

  • Trend Analysis: The chart highlights fluctuations in daily COVID-19 data. Spikes often represent consolidated reporting from weekends or holidays rather than single-day surges.
  • Vaccination Impact: Comparing case trends with vaccination rollouts can reveal visual correlations, though temporal lags and variants must be considered.
  • Design Choices:
    • Color Scale: Dark mode is used to reduce eye strain and emphasize data lines.
    • Smoothing: Data preparation included 7-day rolling means to reveal underlying trends amidst noisy daily reports.
    • Uncertainty: Missing values were filled with zero to avoid line breaks, representing the absence of reported data for those periods.

Important Note: Correlation is not causation. Visual trends should not be used for medical or policy conclusions without rigorous statistical modeling.
